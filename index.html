About Music Mantra

We share love for music with people across the globe, and making learning fun through online classes.

Music Mantra launched the first online music Academy in March 2019. vision for the Academy is to share love for music with people across the globe and to make learning Logical music easy and fun. The Music Manta is a music academy founded by Mr. Ratnesh Kumar, 15 Years of worldwide experience. Academy uses Cloud On - a Personalized Learning Platform that powers online learning.

In a short span of 2 years, academy has built a presence with students from many countries with nearly 100 students. The Academy operates its online classes with live teachers through cutting edge video conferencing technology.
